Social Post Guidance

  • Use dark background `#050505` for feed consistency.
  • CTAs in `#00f2ff` to stand out against dark imagery.
  • Headlines: Inter Bold. Meta/labels: JetBrains Mono.
  • Maintain 24–36px left/right padding for mobile readability.
